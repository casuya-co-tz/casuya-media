## 🎯 Mission

Creating an open-source education platform that works on low-end devices and unreliable internet connections. Built for schools in developing regions.

## 🏗️ Architecture

```
Phase 1 (Foundation)          Phase 2 (Features)
├── casuya-platform           ├── casuya-editor
├── casuya-core               ├── casuya-search
├── casuya-runtime            ├── casuya-media
├── casuya-bridge             ├── casuya-exams
└── casuya-auth               └── casuya-ai
```
